About me

Patricia Quessada

My name is Patricia Quessada

I am French, my last name is Spanish because my grand-father was from Spain. I am a mother of two children who have also the Dutch nationality, which is a beautiful diversity. At 19 years old, after my baccalaureate and full of my natural enthusiasm, I followed my heart and my handsome Dutch boyfriend in the Netherlands. After 25 years in this country, my life reached an incredible turning point: I went through a divorce and lost my job at the French bank at the same time. After a period of suffering and mourning, I recovered thanks to the love I was surrounded with. The experiences I had generated a transformation in me and brought me a lot of insights, wisdom and a new art of living (way of life). I decided to follow a training to become coach and I am now a certified. My diploma is also recognized by the Dutch ministry of education. I really wished to be able to serve as a kind of guide, I have been too many places, sometimes painful and came back stronger. It is why it became very important to me to show the path to the light to the persons who need it and to support them in their quest, in their pursuit of happiness. Each of us I think has to become the person he really is and regain his autonomy in order to leave peacefully. And as Antoine de Saint-Exupéry wrote:‘’On ne voit bien qu’avec le coeur” translated in english “One sees clearly only with the Heart.”
