About me

Patricia Quessada

My name is Patricia Quessada

I am French, my last name is Spanish because my grand-father was. I am a mother of two children who got also de Dutch nationality, a beautiful diversity. At 19 years old, after my baccalaureate and full of my natural enthusiasm I followed my heart and my handsome Dutch boyfriend. When 25 years in this country my life got an incredible turning point, a divorce and the lost of my job at the French bank followed at the same time. After a period of suffering and mourning, I regained the upper hand also thanks to the love I was surrounded with. The experiences I had generated a transformation into me and brought me a lot of insights, wisdom and a new art of living (way of life). I decided to follow a training to become coach and I am now a certified one, my diploma is also recognized by the Dutch ministry of education. I really wished to be able to serve as a kind of guide, I have been too many places, sometime painful and came back stronger. It is why it became very important to me to light up with my lantern the persons who need it and to support them in their quest, in their pursuit of happiness. Each of us I think has to become the person he really is and regain his autonomy in order to leave peacefully. And as Antoine de Saint-Exupéry wrote: ‘’On ne voit bien qu’avec le coeur” translated in english “One sees clearly only with the Heart”
